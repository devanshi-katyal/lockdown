your time starts NOW

21 Days, 21 things to try.We've decided to bring you ideas of the simple things you can do during a lockdown to connect with yourself and with your loved ones. Our lockdown manual is filled with simple pleasures we've forgotten about or don't have enough time for in our daily hustle but are free to explore now.

Stop worrying about your tensions

Worrying about your tensions doesnt make it any less. KEEP CALM AND STAY SAFE. Explore variety of meditations and letting go excercises.

Be a better version of yourself

Explore a long lost hobby. It can be anything, from gardening to crafts. Taking care of something might take off your mind of your problems. Just explore if you cant find any!.

TRY READING!

They say a reader lives a thousand lives before dying, pick up a great novel and a cup of coffee. snuggle into your bed and start reading. Dont know wich book to start with? dont worry we have got you covered, find your next great book with our "BOOK FINDER" tool.

Move that body

Sitting and not working out is very harmful for your body! get out of your bed and move that body with specially curated excercises only for YOU.

Unleash the inner chef

TRY cooking amazing recipes now since you cant order in. View the list of easy to make dishes to satisfy your hunger!

Show gratitude

Be thankful to your friends and family who were there with you in this time of crisis. send them a text message or may be a cute video letter! try and see how much better it will make you feel.

Stay at home and try new styles

try cutting your own hair in home, or maybe try that dress you were hesitant to try on. while stay at home and dress blandly? Try something funky and show your SWAG!

If its your birthday, try baking cake for yourself!

What better birthday gift to ourself than a cake specially suited to your tastebuds! take a look at some of the best in home cake recipes.

Who are we?

We are committed for you to be in your best health, both mental and physical health, today and always!
